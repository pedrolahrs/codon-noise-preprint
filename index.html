Engineering Protein Expression Noise via Synonymous Codon Sequence Design: A Predictive Computational Approach using a Simplified Model

Author: Pedro Henrique Lahr

Published: April 16, 2025

DOI: 10.31219/osf.io/dwgzn_v1

Abstract

This study presents a predictive computational model for controlling gene expression noise through synonymous codon sequence design. By adjusting codon order without altering amino acid sequences, the approach allows for modulating stochastic variability in protein expression. The simplified model demonstrates how silent mutations can significantly affect noise levels, offering new insights for synthetic biology, genetic engineering, and fine-tuning cellular behavior.
